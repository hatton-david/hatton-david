Week 3: Weather Site Project Planning Document

Target Audience

The target audience for this website would be people who love to either have people over or go to places where outdoor food cooking activities are planned. It gives them a place to check weather conditions before the activity is scheduled. The website will ultamatly tell them whether it would be a good idea to have the activity on that day, or plan for another.


Many scenarios could present themselves to make this website useful to my target audience.

- That pony ride at your daughters birthday party would not be ideal when everyone is sopping wet.

- Bad weather would certainly dampen the mood when you are camping with your friends on spring break.

- Friendly games of football or soccer would be much more difficult when you are covered in mud.

- You will know when to put chains on those tires in preperation for the snow.

- You won't get struck by lightning while you are on vacation swimming in the ocean when a storm rolls in.


Here we se a civilized party, properly prepared for the weather.

Think how sad everyone would have been if bad weather had ruined the festivities!
