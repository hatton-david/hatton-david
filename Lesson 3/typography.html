Typography

I chose two basic types of font in relation to typography. For all the headings, I chose an Arial, sans-serif font. This font is modern and when bold it stands out, catching the readers attention.

For all the paragraphs I chose to go with Times New Roman, Serif. I chose this beacause Times New Roman Is a very popular font, and is known to be one of the easies fonts to read.
