Activities

Here are the activities that we offer here at Salmon River Adventure We want to create an environment where you and your Family can learn about nature and the outdoors and still have fun in the process. See below for a brief description of each activity we offer.

Rafting

Come feel the excitement of rushing down the river with the quick current pulling you forward, and the thrill of being tossed back and forth by the white water rapids.

Panning for Gold

A fun activity for the Family. See how early settlers made a living from pulling gold straight from the riverbed. Fun for any age.

Fishing

We offer our river to the public for fishing. It is available until nightfall. You must bring your own equiptment or you can purchase them in the giftshop. It is catch and release fishing. Please do not take the fish from the river.
